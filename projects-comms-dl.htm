Demokrasi Lab

Graphic Design
Branding
Web Design
Social Media Management

I handled the overall design and aesthetic direction of the Demokrasi Lab programme. Purple and grey were fixed as the theme colour but I was given the freedom to establish the overall mood and direction of the programme's design collaterals. I also created a website to promote the programme.

Logo

Inspired by the title of the programme and its nature being a competition for innovative solutions, I decided to incorporate scientific elements into the logo, as represented by the conical flask which doubles up as the letter 'A' for the title.

I also came up with the tagline 'Innovate Democracy' which ties back to the theme of the competition.
